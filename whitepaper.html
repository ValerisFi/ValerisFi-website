Valeris (VRS) Documentation

Last updated: 2026

Overview

Valeris (VRS) is a fair-launch, community-driven token deployed on Solana using pump.fun. There was no presale, no private allocation, and no VC access.

Launch Method

  • Platform: pump.fun
  • Launch Type: Fair launch
  • Migration: Automatic to PumpSwap

Tokenomics

Token Name: Valeris

Ticker: VRS

Blockchain: Solana

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 (1B)

Distribution

  • Public (pump.fun buyers): 97%
  • Team / Dev: 2 - 5%

No tokens will be minted after launch. No hidden wallets or private allocations will exist.

Taxes

  • Buy Tax: 0%
  • Sell Tax: 0%
  • Transfer Tax: 0%

Authorities & Contract Safety

pump.fun enforces the following automatically at launch:

  • Mint Authority: Renounced
  • Freeze Authority: Revoked

This ensures:

  • No additional tokens can ever be minted
  • No wallets can be frozen
  • Supply is permanently fixed

These settings are publicly verifiable on Solscan.

Liquidity

  • Liquidity is generated automatically during the pump.fun migration
  • Liquidity is paired on PumpSwap

Liquidity Policy

LP tokens will be burned or locked after migration. This prevents liquidity removal and protects holders.

Proof transaction will be shared publicly once completed.

Market Transparency

  • Trading occurs on PumpSwap
  • Charts are visible on DexScreener and Birdeye

The chart will display:
“Migrated from Pump v1”

This indicates the token successfully completed the pump.fun lifecycle.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1 – Launch & community formation
  • Phase 2 – Migration to PumpSwap
  • Phase 3 – Community-driven growth
